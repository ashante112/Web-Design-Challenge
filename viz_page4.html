City Latitude vs. Wind Speed

This plot depicts the city latitude and wind speed. Concentrations of windspeed are normally distributed across the latitude span with a few notable outliers occuring in the highest latitude region (50-80N). Proportionally, the bulk of the windspeed data fall below the 10mph measurement, with a more significant concentration falling below the 5mph measurement across the range of latitudes.
