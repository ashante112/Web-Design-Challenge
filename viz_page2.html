Humidity

This visualization depicts the correlation between latitude and humidity. The cities in this dataset show no direct correlation with humidity and their latitude. A key observation is that the majority of cities have a humidity percentage that is 60% or greater. Another notable observation is that while the data contains significant samples of cities that have 100% humidity spanning the entire latitude range, while conversely, their is a cluster of samples with 0% humidity that only fall in the highest latitude range, namely the coldest region on the earth.
