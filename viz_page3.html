Cloudiness

This plot depicts the correlation between city latitude and cloudiness. No direct correlation exists, although it is easy to observe significant clusters of citites that have 0% humidity across all latitudes. Significant clusters also fall in the 75-90% cloudiness range, however, these fall primarily in the 30-70N latitude range.
