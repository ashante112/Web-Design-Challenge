Max Temperature (F)

This chart displays the correlation between the city latitude and max temperature. As represented in this visualization, the max temperatures tend to trend closer to the equator at 0 Latitude. At the quator, the max temperature shows a more concentrated trend around 75 degrees F. As the Latitude increases, the temperature shows a gradual decrease, with the most extreme lows plotting in the highest latitudes of 80N-90N. On the other end, the temperature similarly decreases as the latitude decreases, but not the extent of the extreme temperatures seen in the highest latitudes.
