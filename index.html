Analysis

On this site, you will find a number of plots that are used to analyze the weather along various points around the globe using the locational determinant of latitude. In our analysis, we use data from 546 cities around the world, comparing their collective measurements in the following areas:

  • Max temperature (F)
  • Humidity
  • Coudiness
  • Wind Speed

You will find a page where you can visually compare all plot results for these measurements, as well as seeing a more granular view of the original data and its structure.
